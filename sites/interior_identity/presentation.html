Interior Identity

This website would serve the purpose of displaying interior design photography and also architecture photography in a dynamic format to connect its viewer to the space. The fullscreen gallery aspect distances the user from the feeling of their web browser and closer to the sites content. Combined with the subtle dimensionality of the sites navigation this will create an emersive feeling for the end user. The target audience is other designers and photographers who are interested in viewing others work for research or inspiration and non designers or photographers who are looking for professionals to hire. The end users will pay strong attention to graphics and general aesthetic choices because the content of the site is based on design. The sites colors will use black and white primarily with varying opacities. The highlight color will be based on the image being displayed. The border's of the text box and the site's logo will be the only color that changes page to page. I will be using Avenir. Avenir is a very modern sans that is basically an updated Helvetica. The sans will match clean and sharp geometry of the interior design photos I will be using.
